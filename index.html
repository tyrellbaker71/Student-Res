About Us

Built for Student Success

Germiston House provides a secure, comfortable living environment designed to help students focus on what truly matters — their education.

Safe & Secure

24/7 on-site security with controlled access ensures every resident feels protected at all times. Your peace of mind is our priority.

Modern Amenities

High-speed WiFi, furnished rooms, communal kitchens, and study spaces — everything a modern student needs to thrive academically and socially.

NSFAS Accredited

Officially accredited by the National Student Financial Aid Scheme. NSFAS-funded students can apply directly — financial worries handled.

Affordable Living

Competitive rates that fit a student budget without compromising on quality or comfort. Transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Prime Location

Very conveniently located approximately 300 metres from EWC - Ekurhuleni West College (TVET) Germiston Campus

Community Feel

Join a vibrant, diverse community of students. Common areas and social spaces foster friendships and a true sense of belonging.
